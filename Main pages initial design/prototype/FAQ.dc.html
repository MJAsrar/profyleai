FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything about credits, privacy, and how the tools fit together.

GENERAL
Do I need to pay to build a résumé?+−

No. Building, editing, and exporting résumés to PDF is free and unlimited. Credits are only spent on the AI steps — tailoring, cover letters, and interview practice.

What makes a template "ATS-optimised"?+−

It uses a single-column, machine-readable layout with standard section headings and no graphics that trip up applicant-tracking software — so your résumé is parsed correctly before a human ever sees it.

Can I use it for more than one job?+−

Yes — keep one base résumé and tailor a fresh version for each posting. Every tailored copy is saved separately in "My résumés".

CREDITS & BILLING
Do credits expire?+−

Never. Credits stay in your account until you use them, whether that's this week or next semester.

What happens if I run out mid-task?+−

We'll pause and offer to top up before spending anything. You're never charged without confirming, and unfinished work is saved.

Is there a subscription?+−

No recurring subscription — you buy credit packs when you need them. Prices shown are placeholders for this draft.

PRIVACY & DATA
Who can see my résumé and answers?+−

Only you. Your documents and interview transcripts are private to your account, and you can export or delete all of your data at any time from Settings.

Do you train AI on my data?+−

Your content isn't used to train models. It's sent to our AI providers only to generate your result, then returned to you.
