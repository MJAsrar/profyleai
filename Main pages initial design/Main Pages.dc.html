Getting started / Quick start

Quick start

Go from an empty account to a job-ready résumé, cover letter, and interview plan in about ten minutes. Here's the whole path.

1. Create your profile

Sign up and you'll get 10 free credits. Add your name, contact details, and a headline once — every tool reuses this profile, so you never re-type it.

2. Build a résumé

Pick one of five ATS-tested templates and fill in each section with the live preview beside you. Building and exporting to PDF is always free.

TIP

Lead every bullet with a verb and end with a result. The "✦ AI" button rewrites weak bullets for you.

3. Point it at a job

Paste a job description once. Tailoring (2 credits) reshapes your résumé to match it and gives you a match score; the cover-letter tool (2 credits) reuses the same job automatically.

screenshot · tailoring result

4. Practise the interview

Generate role-specific questions and rehearse — as text with STAR scoring (5 credits), or live with your voice against an AI interviewer (50 credits).

PRIVACY POLICY

Your data, your career

This policy explains what ProfyleAI collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It's written to be readable — the plain-language summary in each section is the intent; the full terms govern.

Placeholder notice: this is draft copy for design review, not legal advice — your counsel should review the final text.

1. Information we collect

We collect the details you add to your profile (name, contact information, work history), the résumés and documents you create, and basic usage data needed to run the service. We do not buy personal data from third parties.

2. How we use it

Your information is used to provide the features you ask for — building résumés, generating tailored versions and cover letters, and running interview practice — plus to secure your account and process payments. We don't sell your personal information.

3. AI processing

When you use an AI tool, the relevant content is sent to our AI providers only to generate your result, then returned to you. Your content is not used to train their models. Voice interviews are processed to produce a live transcript and feedback for your session.

4. Sharing & providers

We share data only with the service providers that make ProfyleAI work — for example payment processing and AI generation — under agreements that limit them to those purposes. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Your rights & choices

You can view, export, or delete all of your data at any time from Settings → Data. Deleting your account removes your documents and personal information from our active systems.

6. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@profyle.ai or through the contact page.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything about credits, privacy, and how the tools fit together.

GENERAL
Do I need to pay to build a résumé?+−

No. Building, editing, and exporting résumés to PDF is free and unlimited. Credits are only spent on the AI steps — tailoring, cover letters, and interview practice.

What makes a template "ATS-optimised"?+−

It uses a single-column, machine-readable layout with standard section headings and no graphics that trip up applicant-tracking software — so your résumé is parsed correctly before a human ever sees it.

Can I use it for more than one job?+−

Yes — keep one base résumé and tailor a fresh version for each posting. Every tailored copy is saved separately in "My résumés".

CREDITS & BILLING
Do credits expire?+−

Never. Credits stay in your account until you use them, whether that's this week or next semester.

What happens if I run out mid-task?+−

We'll pause and offer to top up before spending anything. You're never charged without confirming, and unfinished work is saved.

Is there a subscription?+−

No recurring subscription — you buy credit packs when you need them. Prices shown are placeholders for this draft.

PRIVACY & DATA
Who can see my résumé and answers?+−

Only you. Your documents and interview transcripts are private to your account, and you can export or delete all of your data at any time from Settings.

Do you train AI on my data?+−

Your content isn't used to train models. It's sent to our AI providers only to generate your result, then returned to you.
