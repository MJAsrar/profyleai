PRIVACY POLICY

Your data, your career

This policy explains what ProfyleAI collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It's written to be readable — the plain-language summary in each section is the intent; the full terms govern.

Placeholder notice: this is draft copy for design review, not legal advice — your counsel should review the final text.

1. Information we collect

We collect the details you add to your profile (name, contact information, work history), the résumés and documents you create, and basic usage data needed to run the service. We do not buy personal data from third parties.

2. How we use it

Your information is used to provide the features you ask for — building résumés, generating tailored versions and cover letters, and running interview practice — plus to secure your account and process payments. We don't sell your personal information.

3. AI processing

When you use an AI tool, the relevant content is sent to our AI providers only to generate your result, then returned to you. Your content is not used to train their models. Voice interviews are processed to produce a live transcript and feedback for your session.

4. Sharing & providers

We share data only with the service providers that make ProfyleAI work — for example payment processing and AI generation — under agreements that limit them to those purposes. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Your rights & choices

You can view, export, or delete all of your data at any time from Settings → Data. Deleting your account removes your documents and personal information from our active systems.

6. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@profyle.ai or through the contact page.
